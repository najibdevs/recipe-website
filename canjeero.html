Canjeero (Somali pancake)

Description

Canjeero, a staple in Somali cuisine, is a thin, spongy pancake made from a fermented batter of sorghum or teff flour.

Characterized by its slightly sour taste and unique texture, it often accompanies various dishes, serving as both a utensil and complement to meals.

Comparable to Ethiopia's injera, Canjeero holds a distinct place in Somali culinary traditions.
